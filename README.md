# Product Feature Analysis & A/B Testing

End-to-end product analytics project simulating the work of a product analyst
in a mass digital product.

---

## Problem

Evaluate the impact of a new product feature on key business metrics
and decide whether the feature should be rolled out.

---

## Approach

1. Product hypothesis formulation
2. Metric selection:
   - Conversion
   - Retention
3. A/B test design:
   - Target uplift: +2â€“3%
   - Minimum Detectable Effect (MDE): 1.5%
4. Synthetic data generation
5. Statistical analysis:
   - t-test
   - bootstrap
   - confidence intervals
6. Interpretation of results and business recommendations

---

## Key Focus

- Decision-making under uncertainty
- Trade-offs between statistical significance and business impact
- Clear product conclusions instead of pure statistical output
